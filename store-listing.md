## Description (4000 chars max)

> GALE is a one-touch sky-sailing runner about momentum.
>
> Hold to fire your tether at a floating anchor and swing. Release at just
> the right moment to slingshot forward — nail the angle and a PERFECT
> release rockets you across the sky. Skim the cloud sea to charge your wind
> gauge, then unleash a dash that smashes straight through debris.
>
> The world drifts from dawn to dusk to starry night as you fly: snowy
> peaks, pine forests, ancient ruins, monuments, and floating islands with
> their own tiny wildlife. Every run is procedurally scattered — no two
> routes are the same.
>
> FEATURES
> • One-touch play: hold to swing, release to slingshot
> • PERFECT releases, cloud skimming, and wind dashes to master
> • A living day/night cycle — dawn, golden hour, dusk, and starlit night
> • Ever-changing biomes: forests, peaks, ruins, monuments, floating isles
> • Three pilots to unlock your style: Paper Plane, Monkey, and Nyan Cat
> • Run objectives to chase and personal bests to beat
> • No ads. No accounts. No data collection. Just flight.
>
> Easy to pick up, hard to put down — one more run is always calling.
