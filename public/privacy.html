GALE — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

GALE is a sky-sailing endless runner. This policy covers both the GALE iOS app and the web version of the game at this site.

iOS app: no data collected

The GALE iOS app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. Your scores, best runs, and settings are saved only on your device and never leave it. Deleting the app deletes that data.

Web version: advertising

The browser version of GALE on this website shows ads served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies or device identifiers to serve and measure ads, including (where you have consented) personalised ads. See how Google uses information from sites that use its services and Google's Privacy Policy. You can control personalised advertising at Google Ads Settings. The game itself collects nothing; progress is stored locally in your browser (localStorage).

Children

GALE does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. The iOS app contains no ads and no external links beyond this policy.
